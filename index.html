About Tempo's

Tempo's is an open-source room management solution.

With the physical access and home automation oriented features, Tempo's allows you to send reservations in real-time to controllers and allow users to enter into the desired room during the reservation period.

Simplicity

Tempo's offers your users a simple and intuitive interface allowing, in just a few clicks, to book a room.

Administration side, Tempo's offers a flexible and powerful interface to manage all cases.

Security

The Tempo's security system has been thought since the beginning. The project was focused on confidentiality.

You have the complete control over all information accessible to your users.

Efficiency

Managing hundreds of users (or more!) is often tedious. Tempo's makes this management as efficient and pleasant as possible through a very comprehensive set of tools.

No more long hours to individually manage each users, Tempo's does it for you by letting you worry more interesting tasks.

Modernity

The appearance of a system often reflects the image people have of you. Providing Tempo's to your users, you give them a modern and efficient tool.

Your users are satisfied, they talk about it: you win in both image and popularity.
